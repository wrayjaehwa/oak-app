Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Contact: info@oakco.app

Information We May Collect

Oak may collect your name, username, email, age confirmation, state, reviews, ratings, photos, product submissions, dispensary selections, app activity, location if you allow it, feedback, bug reports, support messages, and basic device or usage information.

How We Use Information

We use this information to run Oak, manage accounts, show product and dispensary content, improve search and recommendations, fix bugs, prevent abuse, moderate content, contact beta users, and improve the app.

App-Related Communications

Oak may contact you by email or in-app message about your account, reviews, reports, product submissions, moderation decisions, support requests, security issues, legal updates, app changes, and other app-related notices. These messages help us operate, support, and protect the app.

Public Content

Reviews, ratings, usernames, photos, product submissions, and other content you post may be visible to other users. Please do not post anything you would not want other users to see.

Location

If you allow location access, Oak may use it to show nearby dispensaries, products, or app features. You can control location access in your device settings.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted tools or service providers to help operate Oak, such as hosting, storage, analytics, app development, email, support, bug tracking, moderation, or security tools. We may also share information if required by law or needed to protect Oak, users, public safety, or the platform.

Cannabis-Related Activity

Because Oak is about cannabis products, your use of the app may show cannabis-related interests. Please use the app with that in mind.

Retention and Deletion

We keep information as long as needed to operate Oak, maintain accounts, improve the app, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or maintain business records. You can contact us to ask about your account information or request deletion. Some information may remain if needed for legal, security, backup, moderation, or recordkeeping reasons.
