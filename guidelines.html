Community Guidelines

Oak works best when users share honest, helpful, and respectful cannabis product experiences.

Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Contact: info@oakco.app

Be Honest

Only review products based on your real experience. Do not post fake, copied, paid, or misleading reviews. If you work for a brand or dispensary, received a free product, or were paid or incentivized to post, clearly say that.

Be Respectful

Do not harass, threaten, bully, shame, attack others, or post hateful or abusive content.

No Illegal Sales

Do not use Oak to buy, sell, trade, ship, deliver, or arrange cannabis outside licensed legal channels. Oak is not a marketplace and does not sell cannabis.

No Medical Claims

You can share your personal experience, but do not claim that a product cures, treats, prevents, or diagnoses a medical condition. Do not tell users to use cannabis instead of medical care or professional advice.

Keep It Safe

Do not post private information, content involving minors, illegal activity, unsafe behavior, spam, misleading promotions, or content you do not have the right to share.

Moderation

Oak may remove posts, limit features, or suspend accounts that break these guidelines or create risk for the community.

Questions? Contact us at info@oakco.app.
